Contact & Organization

The Erasmus Department at Institut CAR Sant Cugat is responsible for coordinating the student mobility project and supporting participants throughout the process.

Erasmus Department

The Erasmus Department of CAR High School is composed of the Erasmus Coordinator, the Director of the Institute and the Sports Department Coordinator. All of them are also teachers at the institution, so they have a direct understanding of the students’ academic and personal needs.

The student mobility project is aimed at students enrolled in the two-year Physical Education degree. All students are adults and must complete 410 hours of in-company training in order to obtain their qualification.

The Erasmus Coordinator has specific duties related to student mobility, and part of the weekly timetable is allocated to this work. Although the coordinator is the main person responsible for the project, decisions are shared with the rest of the Erasmus Department.

In the longer term, as the school expands student mobility opportunities, more teachers will take part in the decision-making process in order to help identify the most suitable destination or internship placement for each applicant.

Communication

Communication with partners, students and institutions can take place by email, telephone, video conference or any other suitable format that ensures effective coordination. The Erasmus Department remains open to different communication channels depending on the needs of each collaboration.
