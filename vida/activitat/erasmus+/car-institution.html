Erasmus+ at the “Centre d’Alt Rendiment Esportiu” Institution

Our international strategy focuses on offering meaningful internship opportunities abroad to students in higher education sports and physical education programmes.

Institutional framework

Although our institution offers several educational programmes, from secondary education to post-compulsory studies, the first stage of our international strategy is focused on helping higher education students find suitable internship placements and partner organisations abroad.

The higher education programme currently involved in this strategy is related to sports activities and physical education.

Our main objective is to provide students with an internship abroad that allows them to deepen and broaden the skills they have developed during their studies. At present, we focus on internship mobility rather than study mobility, given our limited previous experience in sending students abroad.

In the medium and long term, and depending on the needs of our students and the evolution of our network of international partners, this strategy could gradually grow into a broader mobility programme.

Profile of partner organisations

Since our students are enrolled in Higher VET studies in Physical Education, future partners should be organisations related to sport, physical activity or fitness.

It is essential that these organisations host the student as a trainee and not as a substitute for an employee. The student remains in a learning process and therefore needs guidance, supervision and evaluation throughout the internship period.

In this context, the partner organisation must be able to act as mentor, evaluator and professional reference for the student.

Possible professional fields

Sports instruction

Students may work as sports instructors in sports centres, summer camps, hotels or related environments.

Fitness and wellbeing

They may also develop their internship in gyms, fitness centres or sports clubs as fitness instructors.

Sports administration

Another possible field is the sports administration department of a company or sports organisation.

European outlook

The initial stage of the project is focused on finding partners in Europe. Language may represent a challenge, since most of our students only speak English or French as foreign languages.

Even so, completing an internship abroad offers students a valuable personal and professional experience. It gives them the opportunity to discover different ways of working, continue learning in real contexts and enrich their training with an added international dimension.
