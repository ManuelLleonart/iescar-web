Apply for Erasmus+ at Institut del CAR

Erasmus+ offers Upper VET students in Physical Education the opportunity to complete their internship abroad and gain valuable international experience.

Purpose of the programme

The aim of the Erasmus+ project is to allow students in the Upper VET course of Physical Education to carry out their internship abroad, broaden their professional experience and become familiar with other ways of working.

Application process

Students interested in taking part in the project must submit an application to the Erasmus Department. All requests are reviewed and assessed by the department.

In order to participate, students must have passed all their subjects before the mobility period begins.

Applicants are also required to attend an interview, where they must demonstrate their readiness and ability to complete their internship abroad successfully.

Selection criteria

Academic progress

Students must have successfully completed all required subjects before departure.

Application

A formal application to the Erasmus Department is required in order to be considered.

Interview

Students must show that they are prepared to undertake an international internship in a professional environment.

Follow-up and support

Throughout the internship period, the aim is to remain in close contact with Erasmus students, their mentors and the staff of the host company.

Video conferences and other communication tools will be used whenever necessary in order to ensure proper follow-up and support during the mobility.
